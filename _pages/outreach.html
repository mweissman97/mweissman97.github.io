History

I really like coloring. So much so that, in 2013, I drew my first coloring booklet. Since then, I have distributed thousands of physical copies of my books in public schools across the country. I plan on continuing to use what I have learned throughout my own journey to use art to inspire the next generation of scientists, communicate ideas in my own research to a larger audience, and be the representation I wished for as kid.
